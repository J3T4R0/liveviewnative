# ZStack

`<z-stack>`, lays out children on top of each other, from back to front.

## Attributes

- `alignment`: How views within this stack are aligned.
    - `top-leading`
    - `top`
    - `top-trailing`
    - `leading`
    - `center` (default)
    - `trailing`
    - `bottom-leading`
    - `bottom`
    - `bottom-trailing`
    - `leading-last-text-baseline`
    - `trailing-last-text-baseline`
